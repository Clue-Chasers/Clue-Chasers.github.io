    Trimmed Completionist Requirement Guide

    Trimmed Completionist Cape Requirement Guide (Courtesy of #Unknown user)

    ### Introduction

    This guide is designed for experienced players aiming to acquire the complete Globetrotter outfit. It assumes that you have already completed all the necessary quests and achievement diaries. Specific sections offer supplementary information to aid you further. However, if your primary goal is to optimize presets, you can find semi-advanced presets for Hards, Elites, and Masters towards the end of this guide. These presets may incur some additional costs compared to a Beginner preset, but not the maximum cost (e.g., using 1 Passage of the Abyss instead of 2).

    ​

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ For a comprehensive overview on clues, you can refer to #unknown-channel.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ If you wish to compare the cost analysis of using skips versus not using skips, utilize the command !casket in #unknown-channel.

    We welcome any suggestions for improving this guide; please share your ideas with us in the #unknown-channel channel.

    Should you require advice or have any questions, don't hesitate to make use of the #unknown-channel channel.

    ​

    ​

    To efficiently accumulate Treasure Trail points for the full Globetrotter , you will need to complete the following number of clues:

    ​

    1000 Hard Clues

    750 Hard Clues + 69 Master Clues

    500 Hard Clues + 120 Elite Clues + 74 Master Clues

    500 Elite Clues

    350 Elite Clues + 93 Master Clues

    ​

    *Note: The Master clue numbers assume the average drop rate of from reward caskets.

    If you already have Master clues, prioritize completing those first. Then, use the Treasure Trail point calculator to determine the remaining points required.

    ​

    Should you consider a specific Clue tier or not?

    ⬥ Easy clue scrolls offer very little Treasure Trail points and are not worth doing to obtain the outfit.

    ⬥ Medium clue scrolls require the Way of the Foot Shaped Key reward to be efficient and this is not worth purchasing prior to the outfit.

    ⬥ Master clue scrolls are an incredible source of Treasure Trail points and should be done as much as possible due to their speed vs reward point potential.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• You can use costume skipping tickets to bypass the need for hard-to-obtain items.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• The average Master reward greatly outweighs the cost of a Costume Skipping Ticket .

    ​

    > What is the quickest method?

    ⬥ The outcome largely hinges on your speed in solving each clue tier.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• You can determine your points per hour for each tier, considering your own solving pace, using this sheet: #unknown-channel.

    ​

    ⬥Please take note: The Master drop rate (after re-rolls) from Hard clues is 1/11.25, and from Elite clues, it's 1/3.75.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• So, while Hard clues might appear to yield better points per hour compared to Elites, be sure to factor in the number of Masters you obtain as well.
