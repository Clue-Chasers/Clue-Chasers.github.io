    Requirements For Optimal Elite Clues

    Optimal Requirements Elite Clues

    ## Area tasks

    Elite Desert

    ⬥ Unlimited teleports to the Ruins of Uzer and Nardah using the Desert Amulet 4.

    Elite Fremennik

    ⬥ Unlimited teleports to the Rellekka marketplace on Fremmenik Sea Boots and Enchanted Lyre.

    ⬥ Fremennik Isles on the Enchanted Lyre.

    ⬥ Ability to toggle the lyre teleport destination to Jatizso, Neitiznot, Miscellania or Etceteria by speaking to Olaf the Bard.

    Hard Tirannwn

    ⬥ No longer fail stick traps using the Tirannwn Quiver 3.

    ⬥ Additional unlimited teleport locations: Elf Camp and Tirannwn mushroom patch.

    ## Skills

    Max Cape

    ⬥ Offers three perk slots that can be filled with Capes of Accomplishment by using them on it, as well as the functionality to view and remove existing perks.

    ⬥ Max cape perks apply to backpack allowing you to utilize the globetrotter backpack , Dungeoneering Cape , Slayer Cape Quest Cape all at once.

    Archaeology

    ⬥ Level requirement for Abyssal Link. requires level 112. (can be boosted)

    Shadow's Grace

    ⬥ Level requirement for Shadow's Grace. requires level 67. (can be boosted)

    ## Quests

    Evil Dave’s Big Day Out

    ⬥ Ability to chip teleport tablets to alternate teleport destination by using the tablet on Dave's spellbook.

    ⬥ Dave's Spellbook that holds up to 1000 charges of chipped teleports).

    Fur ‘n’ Seek + Rag and Bone Man Wish List

    ⬥ Access to the Skeletal horror Teleport spell.

    Darkness of Hallowvale

    ⬥ Ability to unlock the Burgh de Rott teleport on the Games Necklace with 500 Companion levels from Temple Trekking.

    Desperate Times

    ⬥ Charos’ Clue Carrier

    Dream Mentor

    ⬥ Access to Spellbook Swap.

    King of the Dwarves

    ⬥ For access to the Lava Flow Mine.

    The Dig Site + Find clean necklace in the Varrock Museum

    ⬥ Unlocks Dig Site Pendant.

    Love Story ⬥

    ⬥ Ability to make Teleport to house (chipped), allowing players to teleport to any one of the POH portals.

    The Jack of Spades

    ⬥ Access to Menaphos and ability to relocate the Player-owned-house there.

    Ghost ahoy

    ⬥ The ectophial , which provides a reusable one-click teleport to the Ectofuntus.

    Succession

    ⬥ Access to the Dive ability when Bladed Dive is unavailable.

    ⬥ Tier 6 of the Infernal Puzzle Box becomes available and ability to add to the tool belt.

    Quest cape

    ⬥ This cape provides teleports to the following locations:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Ancient Guthix Temple

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Behind the Scenes

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Champions' Guild

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ The Empty Throne Room

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Glacor Cave

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Fountain of Heroes

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Legends' Guild

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Tears of Guthix

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ Varrock Museum

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ World Gate

    ## Other

    Full Globetrotter outfit

    ⬥ The globetrotter outfit is an outfit that is obtainable from the Treasure Trails Reward Shop with points obtained by completing Treasure Trails.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ The outfit will provide several QoL features some of which are charged-based through solving clues.

    Double Surge ability

    ⬥ An item that can be read to unlock an upgrade to the Surge ability, giving it an extra charge with a separate independent and hidden cooldown.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ The double Surge ability is not functional in the Wilderness when opted into PvP.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ It can be created with 750 codex pages using the lectern. It is consumed upon use.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎⬩ This version can be bought from or sold to other players or the Grand Exchange, but the untradeable version only requires 500 codex pages.

    Bladed Dive ability

    Totem of Treasure

    ⬥ A totem that decreases both the minimum and maximum number of clue scroll steps by one.

    Total level 500 in Temple Trekking

    ⬥ Unlocks a shortcut to the Nature’s Grotto (total level 100) + Burgh de Rott teleport on games necklace.

    Sceptre of the Gods

    ⬥ The Sceptre of the gods is an item that may be received when playing Pyramid Plunder.

    ⬥ It teleports to the same locations as the regular pharaoh's sceptre but has an additional teleport to the Golden Palace in Menaphos.

    ⬥ The sceptre has 10 teleport charges when fully charged, which is increased to 20 with medium Desert achievements completed, and further to 60 with hard Desert achievements completed.

    Nature's Sentinel Outfit

    Master archaeologist's outfit

    Repair the CJS Fairy Ring

    Unlock ancient invention

    Plant a Spirit Tree in Brimhaven
