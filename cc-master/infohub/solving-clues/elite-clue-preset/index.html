    Elite Clue Preset Guide ‎ ‎(Courtesy of #Unknown user)

    Introduction

    The below presets will cover both a non-skip ticket and skip ticket setup, as well as an alternative preset for when your Sentinel Outfit runs out/if you dont want to use the Sentinel Outfit. Presets also assume you are using whilst solving. If you aren't, replace and with items of your choice.

    To see the cost analysis of no skips vs. using skips, use the command !casket in #unknown-channel.

    Any questions regarding the presets, ask in #unknown-channel.

    Improvements and suggestions are always welcome. Post any you may have in #unknown-channel

    Tips & Tricks

    In-game sounds are able to assist with scans. Listening to the pulse instead of staring at your feet allows you focus on surroundings and get information as you are teleporting from one place to another.

    ​

    ⬥ Use Totem of Summoning on Anachronia for an extended meerkat timer

    ⬥ You can right click → modify tabs as you see fit to teleport to other house portals

    ⬥ Activating the globetrotter jacket during a Wilderness Crater scan will show you the level it is at, narrowing down your search route

    ⬥ For the North Eastern Daemonheim spot - Either use Dragonkin Laboratory Tokens and teleport to The Shadow Reef or set your War's Retreat portal to The Shadow Reef

    ⬥ Disassemble Dorgeshkaan Spheres (stackable and cheap) with Scripture of Bik equipped to receive Catalyst of Alteration procs

    ⬥ Use Lantadyme Incense sticks to extend your Spirit Attraction Potion timer from 6 to 8 minutes.

    ⬥ If you run out of Nature Sentinel charges, swap to the BIS Preset (No Sentinel)

    ⬥ For the "No Sceptre of the Gods w/o Skips" preset, use and run the Lumbridge Swamp Cave scan in the dark if you have too.
