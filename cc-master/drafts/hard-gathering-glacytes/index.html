    Hard Gathering Glacytes

    (courtesy of #Saltea)

    Introduction

    Hard clues are fast to gather and solve. They have a master clue scroll rate of 9/100 and have a high Fortunate component rate of 57/100, note that this rate does include re-rolls and tier 4 luck .

    ​

    The meta for gathering hard clues is to kill Glayctes. You can gather an average of 44 (48.3 with Scripture of Bik ) hard clues an hour. Keep in mind that the soft cap still applies, you would have to gather until you have 25 / 50 and then go solve them.

    ​

    Each Hard clue casket is worth 2,052,000 (277,322 with Puzzle skips ). Advanced cluers can gather and solve up to 24.82+ (28.02+ with Puzzle skips ) hard clues an hour putting them at 50,931,087+ (7,770,572 with Puzzle skips ) an hour.
